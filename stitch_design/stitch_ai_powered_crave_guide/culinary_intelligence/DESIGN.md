## Brand & Style
The design system is engineered to evoke the sensory excitement of dining out while maintaining the sophisticated precision of high-end AI technology. The brand personality is **appetizing, energetic, and premium**, positioning the product as a discerning digital concierge rather than a simple directory.

The aesthetic blends **Modern Corporate** structure with **Glassmorphism** accents. It utilizes clean, expansive whitespace to allow food photography to remain the focal point, supported by vibrant accents that stimulate appetite. AI-driven insights are differentiated through subtle motion cues, soft glows, and "intelligent" containers that feel lighter and more ethereal than standard interface elements.

## Colors
The palette is anchored by a warm coral-red primary, specifically chosen to trigger appetite and energy. This is balanced against a deep charcoal for high-contrast typography and a neutral "Bone" off-white for surfaces to reduce eye strain compared to pure white.

### AI Features & Tokens
- **AI Tint:** For AI-generated reasoning or summaries, use a subtle gradient background: `linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(226, 55, 68, 0.05) 0%, rgba(139, 92, 246, 0.05) 100%)`.
- **AI Sparkle:** Accents and icons related to "Smart Recommendations" use the `ai_accent_hex` (a soft violet) to distinguish machine-learning features from standard user interactions.
- **Dark Mode:** In dark mode, surfaces shift to `#1A1A1A` with the primary red maintaining its vibrancy through a slightly increased saturation.

## Typography
The system uses **Plus Jakarta Sans** for headlines to provide a soft, modern, and welcoming geometric feel. Its high x-height ensures impact even at smaller sizes. For body copy, **Work Sans** provides a grounded, professional readability that handles dense restaurant descriptions and menu data with ease.

- **Weight Usage:** Use ExtraBold (800) for restaurant names and primary headlines to create a premium editorial feel.
- **AI Voice:** Any text generated by the AI (e.g., "Why we think you'll love this...") should use the `ai-recommendation` style with a slight violet tint to separate the algorithm's voice from static data.

## Layout & Spacing
The layout follows a **12-column fluid grid** centered within a maximum container width of 1280px for desktop. Spacing is governed by a strict 8px base unit.

- **Generous Whitespace:** Sections should be separated by `stack-lg` (48px) to maintain a premium, uncluttered aesthetic. 
- **Card Grids:** Restaurant listings should span 3 or 4 columns depending on the importance of the imagery. 
- **Sidebars:** Use a 3-column fixed-width sidebar for filters and AI-persona settings, keeping the remaining 9 columns for the main content feed.

## Elevation & Depth
This design system utilizes **Ambient Shadows** and **Tonal Layers** to create a sense of tactile premium quality.

- **Level 1 (Base):** Neutral Bone surfaces (`#F9F9F7`).
- **Level 2 (Cards):** Pure white background with a soft, diffused shadow: `0px 4px 20px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.04)`.
- **Level 3 (AI Insights/Modals):** A higher elevation with a slight violet-tinted shadow and a 1px border using a light gradient to simulate glass catching the light.
- **Interactions:** Upon hover, cards should lift slightly (translate -4px) and the shadow should become deeper and more diffused.

## Shapes
The shape language is defined by **Rounded** corners to feel approachable and "organic," echoing the shapes of plates and tables.

- **Standard Elements:** Buttons and input fields use 12px (`rounded-md`) corner radii.
- **Container Elements:** Restaurant cards and large modals use 24px (`rounded-xl`) corner radii.
- **AI Tags:** Small "Smart Tags" (e.g., "Great for Dates") use a full pill-shape (999px) to contrast against the more structured rectangular cards.

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Solid `#E23744` with white text. 12px rounded corners. Bold Plus Jakarta Sans.
- **AI Secondary:** A subtle glass-effect button with a `1.5px` border of `ai_accent_hex` and a very faint violet fill.

### Cards (Restaurant)
The primary vessel for information. Feature large-scale imagery (16:9 ratio) with a 24px radius. The footer of the card should contain the restaurant name in `headline-md` and a "Match Score" pill.

### AI Match Score
A custom circular or pill component that shows a percentage. It uses a gradient from `#E23744` to `#8B5CF6`. It should have a subtle "pulse" animation when the page first loads to signify active computation.

### Input Fields
Minimalist with a soft `#F1F1F1` fill. On focus, the border transitions to the primary coral color. Labels are always positioned above the field in `label-bold`.

### Chips & Tags
Used for cuisine types and dietary preferences. Use a light neutral fill (`#EEEEEE`) and 8px rounded corners. If the chip is "AI Suggested," it receives a small sparkle icon prefix.

### Lists
Restaurant details (hours, location) should be presented in clean lists with generous vertical padding (16px) and thin, low-contrast separators (`#F1F1F1`).
